How to Play Block Breaker

  1. Control the paddle: Use your mouse or arrow keys to move the paddle horizontally
  2. Bounce the ball: Keep the ball in play by hitting it with your paddle
  3. Break blocks: Aim the ball to destroy all colored blocks at the top of the screen
  4. Collect power-ups: Watch for special items that fall when certain blocks break
  5. Advance through levels: Clear all blocks to move to more challenging stages

Mastering block breaker requires precision and quick thinking. The ball accelerates as you progress, and special blocks may require multiple hits. Some power-ups give you advantages like longer paddles, multi-balls, or laser beams!
