Spacebar Clicker - The Ultimate Clicking Challenge

Spacebar Clicker is the most addictive clicking game that tests your speed and endurance! The rules are simple but the challenge is real - how many times can you press your spacebar in the given time?

How to Play Spacebar Clicker:

  1. Click the "Start Game" button
  2. When the timer begins, press your spacebar as fast as you can
  3. The game lasts for 30 seconds by default (can be adjusted)
  4. Your score is the total number of successful spacebar presses
  5. Compete with friends for the highest score!

Pro tip: Use multiple fingers for maximum clicking speed! Some players achieve over 300 clicks in 30 seconds - can you beat that?
