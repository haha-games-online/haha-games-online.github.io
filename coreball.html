Coreball: The Fast-Paced Game Taking the World by Storm

Coreball is the exciting new sport that combines elements of soccer, basketball, and handball into one adrenaline-pumping game. Played on a circular court with teams of 5 players each, Coreball emphasizes quick thinking, teamwork, and precision scoring.

How to Play Coreball

  1. The Court: Play takes place on a circular field with a central scoring zone (the "Core")
  2. Scoring: Teams score by getting the ball into the Core (3 points) or through the side hoops (1 point)
  3. Movement: Players can dribble, pass, or carry the ball (maximum 3 steps while holding it)
  4. Defense: Contact is limited - defenders must attempt to intercept or block without fouling
  5. Game Length: Matches consist of four 8-minute quarters with a running clock

What makes Coreball special is its dynamic nature - the circular playing field creates 360-degree gameplay where strategy and spatial awareness are key. The sport is gaining popularity worldwide for its fast pace and accessibility to players of all skill levels.
